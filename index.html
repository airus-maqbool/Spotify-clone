Frequently asked questions

What is spotify?

Spotify is a digital music streaming service that gives users access to millions of songs, podcasts, and videos from artists around the world. It offers both free and premium subscription options, with the premium version providing features like ad-free listening, offline downloads, and better sound quality.

Is spotify 100% free?

Spotify provides a Free version and tiered Premium options, which add features like ad-free listening, personalized recommendations and high-quality audio.

Is spotify good for kids?

Spotify Kids does not contain content marked Explicit, so you can relax and let them explore freely. It is important to ensure that any content you play on a shared device is appropriate for all listeners, especially anyone under 18.
